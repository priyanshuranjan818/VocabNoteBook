# 🇩🇪 VokabelBox — German Vocabulary Learning App

> You write the word in your notebook. A week later, you've forgotten how it sounds. **VokabelBox fixes that.**

---

## What It Does

VokabelBox is a personal German vocabulary companion. You add the words you're actively learning, and the app keeps them alive — with correct pronunciation audio, games to cement them in memory, and a dedicated Artikel trainer so *der, die, das* stops being a nightmare.

No more scribbling words in a notebook only to forget how they sound.

---

## Features

### 🔊 Add Words + Listen to Pronunciation
Add any German word you've encountered and instantly hear it pronounced correctly. Great for learners who study from textbooks or physical notes but need an audio reference on demand.

### 🏠 Home
Your personal dashboard — see your current streak, recently added words, and quick-access shortcuts to all sections.

### ➕ Add Word
Add new vocabulary with a single tap. Each entry stores the word, its meaning, and its Artikel (der / die / das). Audio playback is available immediately after saving.

### 📖 Artikels Section
A dedicated trainer for mastering German grammatical genders — one of the trickiest parts of the language. Drill *der, die, das* until it becomes instinct.

### 🎮 Practice
Games and exercises designed to move words from short-term recall to long-term memory. Keeps studying from feeling like a chore.

### 🔥 Streaks
Stay consistent. Your streak tracks daily activity and keeps you accountable — because vocabulary sticks when you show up every day.

### 📤 Import / Export
Move your word list in and out of the app freely. Export to back up your vocabulary or share it with a study partner. Import from a file to get started quickly or restore your list on a new device.

---

## How to Use

1. **Add a word** — Tap the Add Word section and enter the German word, its meaning, and its Artikel.
2. **Hear it** — Play the audio to lock in the correct pronunciation.
3. **Practice** — Use the Practice section to test yourself with games.
4. **Train Artikels** — Head to the Artikels section to drill grammatical genders separately.
5. **Keep your streak alive** — Open the app daily. Check the Streaks section to see your progress.
6. **Back up your list** — Use Export to save your vocabulary. Use Import to restore or load a new word set.
